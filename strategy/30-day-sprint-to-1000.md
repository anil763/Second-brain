# 30-Day Sprint to $1,000

**Goal:** Generate $1,000 in new revenue by March 5, 2026
**Strategy:** Hybrid approach — UGC + Spiritual Services

## Revenue Breakdown

| Source | Target | How |
|--------|--------|-----|
| UGC Brand Deals | $400 | 1-2 small deals or retainers |
| Spiritual Readings | $400 | 8 readings at $50 each |
| Digital Products | $200 | Numerology guide or templates |
| **Total** | **$1,000** | |

## Week 1 (Feb 6-12): Foundation

### Day 1-2 (Thu-Fri): Setup Sprint
- [ ] Create UGC portfolio page on anilcreates.com (3 sample videos)
- [ ] Set up Calendly for numerology readings ($50/30min)
- [ ] Create simple "Life Path Guide" PDF (sell for $17)
- [ ] Update Linktree with offerings

### Day 3-5 (Sat-Mon): Outreach Blitz
- [ ] Send 5 personalized UGC pitches (use template from brand research)
- [ ] Post daily numerology content (use scripts provided)
- [ ] Announce spiritual readings to current followers
- [ ] Engage 30 min/day on target brand accounts (YNAB, Empower, etc.)

### Day 6-7 (Tue-Wed): Content & Systems
- [ ] Film 5 numerology videos in one batch
- [ ] Schedule 7 days of content
- [ ] Set up simple tracking spreadsheet
- [ ] Join 3 Facebook groups for GenX + spirituality

## Week 2 (Feb 13-19): Momentum

### Daily Actions:
- [ ] Post numerology content daily at 7:30am
- [ ] Send 2-3 UGC pitches daily (aim for 20 total this week)
- [ ] Offer 3 free mini-readings to generate testimonials
- [ ] Engage with every comment to boost algorithm

### Milestones:
- First spiritual reading booked
- 3+ responses from brand outreach
- 500+ followers on primary platform

## Week 3 (Feb 20-26): Conversion

### Focus: Turn interest into revenue
- [ ] Follow up on all brand pitches (second email)
- [ ] Launch Life Path Guide ($17) with email to followers
- [ ] Host 1 free "Intro to Numerology" live (build list)
- [ ] Raise reading price to $75 if demand is high

### Content Shift:
- 50% daily energy readings
- 30% educational (Life Path, Personal Year)
- 20% personal story/healing journey

## Week 4 (Feb 27-Mar 5): Close & Scale

### Focus: Hit the number
- [ ] Final push on brand deals (negotiate, don't just accept)
- [ ] Bundle offer: Reading + Guide for $97
- [ ] Ask satisfied clients for referrals
- [ ] Document what's working for next month

## Daily Non-Negotiables

**Morning (7-8am):**
- 7:30am: Post numerology content
- Check/respond to DMs (10 min)

**Midday (12-1pm):**
- Send 2-3 UGC pitches (15 min)
- Engage on target brand posts (15 min)

**Evening (8-9pm):**
- Film tomorrow's content or batch film (30 min)
- Check analytics, adjust strategy (10 min)

## Tracking Dashboard

Create a simple Google Sheet with:
- Daily revenue
- UGC pitches sent/responses/deals
- Readings booked/completed
- Follower growth by platform
- Content performance (views, engagement)

## If Behind After Week 2

**Pivot options:**
1. Lower reading price to $35 temporarily
2. Offer "Pay what you can" for first 5 readings
3. Create urgency: "Only 5 spots this week"
4. Partner with another creator for cross-promotion

## Key Metrics to Watch

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| UGC Response Rate | 20%+ |
| Reading Conversion | 10% of inquiries |
| Content Views | 1,000+ per video |
| Follower Growth | 200+/week |

## Mindset

**You don't need perfection. You need action.**

Every pitch that doesn't get a response is practice.
Every reading you do builds your confidence.
Every video you post builds your presence.

The $1,000 isn't the goal — the *habit of selling* is.
