## What is Second Brain?

Second Brain is a tool for capturing, connecting, and retrieving your ideas. It follows the principles of the Zettelkasten method and Building a Second Brain (BASB) methodology.

## Key Features

### 1. Markdown-First
All your notes are stored as simple markdown files. This means:
- **Portability** - Your data is always yours
- **Longevity** - Plain text lasts forever
- **Flexibility** - Edit with any text editor

### 2. Wiki Links
Connect your thoughts using `[[Wiki Links]]`. This creates a network of knowledge.

For example, check out the [[Zettelkasten Method]] concept page.

3. **Review Regularly** - Your second brain grows with use
4. **Trust the Process** - The connections emerge over time
