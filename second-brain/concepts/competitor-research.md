# Competitor Research & Market Analysis

## Executive Summary

The spiritual coaching and holistic services market is growing significantly, with practitioners charging anywhere from $33 to $10,000+ depending on positioning, experience, and service type. **Anil's $33 numerology reading is positioned at the entry-level, offering significant room for expansion.**

## Pricing Psychology Insights

### What Works in Spiritual Markets

1. **Angel numbers convert well**: $33, $111, $333, $444, $888
2. **Odd numbers feel precise**: $97, $197, $297, $997
3. **Transformation justifies premium**: Focus on outcomes, not hours
4. **Packages outsell singles**: 3-6 session bundles are sweet spot
5. **Payment plans unlock high-ticket**: $997 = $333/month x 3
