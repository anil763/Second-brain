# Lead Magnets & List Building Strategy

## Philosophy

Lead magnets should:
1. **Demonstrate your unique method** (Numerology + Chinese Astrology)
2. **Provide immediate value** (quick win)
3. **Create desire for more** (natural upsell to $33 reading)
4. **Qualify ideal clients** (spiritual seekers wanting deeper meaning)
