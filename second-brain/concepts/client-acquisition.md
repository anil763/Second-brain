# Client Acquisition Strategy

## Overview

Client acquisition for spiritual services relies heavily on **trust and relationship building**. Unlike product businesses, people buy transformation from people they feel connected to. Strategy prioritizes authentic connection over aggressive marketing.

## Referral Strategy

### Make Referrals Easy

1. **Create a referral ask script:**
   "If this reading resonated with you, I'd be so grateful if you'd share it with a friend who might benefit."

2. **Referral incentive program:**
   - Refer a friend = they get $5 off their first reading
   - You get $5 credit toward your next service

3. **Testimonial collection:**
   - Send automated request 3 days after delivery
   - Make it easy (Google form, 2-3 questions)
   - Offer to keep anonymous if preferred

### Referral Sources to Cultivate

- Past clients (biggest source)
- Friends and family (early days)
- Complementary practitioners (astrologers, coaches)
- Online communities (Facebook groups, Reddit)

## Launch Strategy (First 90 Days)

### Week 1-2: Foundation
- [ ] Set up Instagram (or optimize existing)
- [ ] Create ConvertKit account and lead magnet
- [ ] Design simple landing page
- [ ] Announce services to personal network

### Week 3-4: Soft Launch
- [ ] Offer 5 beta readings at $22 (collect testimonials)
- [ ] Post daily on Instagram
- [ ] Start building email list
- [ ] Reach out to 10 potential podcast guests

### Week 5-8: Momentum
- [ ] Increase posting frequency
- [ ] Launch email nurture sequence
- [ ] First podcast appearance (target)
- [ ] Introduce higher-tier offerings

### Week 9-12: Optimization
- [ ] Analyze what's working
- [ ] Double down on top channels
- [ ] Refine messaging based on feedback
- [ ] Set up testimonial flywheel

## Mindset Note

Building a client base takes time. Expect:
- **Month 1-3:** Slow, foundational work
- **Month 4-6:** Momentum building
- **Month 7-12:** Consistent growth
- **Year 2+:** Snowball effect

Trust the process. Consistency beats intensity. Every post, every email, every reading builds toward the future.
