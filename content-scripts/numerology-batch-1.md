## Production Notes

**Filming Tips:**
- Film with warm lighting (golden hour or ring light)
- Use eye contact — numerology is intimate
- Speak slower than you think — people need to absorb this
- Show calculations on screen as text overlays

**Text Overlay Style:**
- Clean, sans-serif font
- High contrast (white on dark, or black on light)
- Keep text minimal — 3-5 words max per overlay

**Posting Schedule:**
- Daily energy reading: 7:30am (your routine)
- Educational content (scripts 1, 2, 4): 2-3x per week
- Personal/real talk (script 5): 1x per week
